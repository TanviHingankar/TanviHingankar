Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices of bacon
  • 8 slices of cheese (your choice)
  • 4 slices of tomato
  • 8 slices of bread
  • Butter or margarine

Instructions:

1. Cook bacon in a skillet over medium heat until crispy. Drain on paper towels and set aside.

2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.

3. Place a slice of cheese on the unbuttered side of 4 slices of bread. Top each with 2 slices of bacon and 1 slice of tomato. Place another slice of cheese on top of each sandwich, then cover with the remaining slices of bread, buttered side facing out.

4. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Place sandwiches in the skillet and cook until golden brown on both sides and cheese is melted, about 3-4 minutes per side.

5. Serve hot and enjoy!
